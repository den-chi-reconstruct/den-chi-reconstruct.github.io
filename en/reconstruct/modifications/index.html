Modifications

Foreign funded enterprise does any modifications on such as enterprise name, registered address, legal person, registered capital, operating period, equity transfer, shareholder’s name/ title, forms of investment, business scope, director and senior managements and by-laws; the enterprise needs to apply for the modifications and/or get an approval from the related authority

The following are the application procedures of modifications in terms of reducing registered capital. Even though the legal ground of reducing registered capital of foreign funded enterprise is clearly stated on the related documents, as far as we are concerned, in practice, authorities strictly examine the reducing registered capital applications.

Regulations

Circular of the Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation and the State Administration for Industry and Commerce on Relevant Provisions and Procedures for Foreign Investment Enterprise Adjusting Total Investment Amount and Registered Capital

Implementing Regulations of the Law of the People's Republic of China on Sino-Foreign Equity Joint Ventures

Implementing Rules for the Law of the People's Republic of China on Wholly Foreign-owned Enterprises

Implementing Rules of the Law of People's Republic of China on Sino-Foreign Cooperative Joint Ventures (Revised in 2014)

General Procedures

Board Resolutions

Applications for source withholding (filing) income tax by outside investors (regarding to dividend distribution) Applications with the former approving authority(Department of Commerce) 

First Application

Announcement made on the newspaper and informs creditors

Second Application

Applications for modifications of Business License with Administration for Industry & Commerce

When it involves modifications of registered information with Taxation and so on, enterprise should go through the application procedures to the related authorities.

Notice

  • When foreign company reduces capital and needs to transfer capital overseas, pay attention to the requirements of Department of Foreign Exchange
  • Tax compliance requirements of Tax Bureau (identify the reprocessed assets)
